ChangeIS — Project Delivery Case Studies

A practical record of how complex systems were improved, stabilized, or rebuilt — using disciplined engineering, clear trade-offs, and measurable outcomes.

What this is

This site is a curated set of real-world project case studies delivered across financial services, security, infrastructure, and regulated environments.

Each case study focuses on what changed, why it mattered, and what was learned — not on tools for their own sake.

How to read the case studies

Every case study is documented using the S.T.A.R.R.Y. format. This keeps the narrative grounded in decisions, constraints, and results.

SSituationThe operational or business context.
TTaskWhat needed to be achieved, and what could not change.
AActionThe concrete steps taken, including architecture and process.
RResultMeasurable outcomes — latency, cost, risk, reliability.
RReturnFinancial or operational impact where quantifiable.
YYieldLong-term leverage: capability, confidence, or optionality gained.

How to explore

Case studies can be browsed and filtered by:

  • Location (APAC, JP, EU, US, Global)
  • Industry
  • Capabilities delivered
  • Results, return, and yield

The intent is not comparison — but pattern recognition.
